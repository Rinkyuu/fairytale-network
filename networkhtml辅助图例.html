Character Functions

H

Hero / Heroine

The protagonist who embarks on a quest and achieves transformation.

V

Villain

Creates harm or opposition to the hero's journey.

D

Donor

Provides magical agents or assistance to overcome obstacles.

L

Helper

Aids the hero in tasks or supports the quest completion.

P

Prince / Princess

Represents the reward, marriage, or restoration of order.

F

False Hero

Claims credit for hero's deeds or attempts to usurp the reward.
